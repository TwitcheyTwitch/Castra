Founding Story

Castra Corporation was established in 2950 by a visionary group of business owners seeking to reshape the landscape of industry and manufacturing.

Frustrated with the monopolistic grip of mega-corporations in systems like Stanton and Terra, they aspired to create an organization that championed self-reliance and cooperative success.
Initially operating out of the bustling industrial environment of ArcCorp (Stanton III), the organization aimed to create a collaborative yet self-reliant manufacturing hub that focused on profitability, innovation, and mutual cooperation.
The founders have their sights set on the Castra system as their future home base due to its strategic location and rich, yet underutilized, resources.
The name "Castra," derived from the ancient term for a military camp or fortress, symbolizes their commitment to building a stronghold of industry and opportunity for those seeking independence and growth through mutual prosperity.

The early days saw Castra Corporation setting up advanced manufacturing facilities and resource extraction operations. Their foundational motto, "Forge Together, Prosper Together," encapsulated their mission to build a profitable environment that nurtured individual growth while advancing collective goals.
