Castra is an organization that focuses on salvage, mining, and logistics. Our members tend to be older than the average player, who prioritizes having fun over making money.

We're a relaxed organization with a laid-back mentality. Most of us have been playing this game for a while. If you're new or looking for a better way to approach something, Castra's members have been there, done that, and wrote a guide about it.

The future is exciting with manufacturing, base building, and exploration. The game is expanding, and we want to expand with it.

If Castra sounds like something that interests you, fill out an application and visit our discord page.
